2. Information We Collect and Storage Mechanisms

IconCopy operates primarily as a client-side utility system. We do not require any user registration, profiling parameters, accounts creation, or the submission of personally identifiable information (PII) such as your full name, physical address, or phone number to browse, access, or utilize our core emoji search engine. The limited datasets processed across our pipelines are classified into the following transparent sectors:

  • Global Aggregate Analytics: To scale our database metrics and show interactive live milestone counters, our system securely tallies global click aggregates (such as the total count of copies processed). This tracking is executing over serverless, zero-identity network nodes, which strictly do not capture, log, or associate your unique IP address or hardware descriptors with the numerical values.
  • Local Sandbox Cache (LocalStorage): Standard user experience features—including your custom visual interface theme (Light Mode versus Dark Mode toggles) and your recently copied history arrays—are completely maintained locally inside your web browser's isolated client sandbox (`LocalStorage`). This data is never synchronized, transferred, or streamed to our servers or any network endpoints; it remains 100% under your localized control.
  • Direct Voluntary Communication: If you contact us directly via email, we may receive additional contextual information about you, such as your email address, the contents of your message, any attachments you choose to send, and any other text details you intentionally provide.

These parameters are fundamentally not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. 9. CCPA Privacy Rights (California Consumer Privacy Act)

Under the statutory compliance directives of the CCPA, consumer demographics originating from California are granted specific transactional rights, which include:

  • Requesting that a business disclosing consumer categories explicitly enumerate the precise elements of personal data collected about consumers.
  • Demanding that an enterprise completely delete any collected personal records stored about the consumer consumer.
  • Demanding that a digital business that sells a consumer's personal data, completely cease selling the consumer's personal data assets immediately.

If you submit a formal request regarding CCPA data deletion or disclosure, our response window is bounded strictly to 30 calendar days. Please connect with our support desk if you choose to exercise these options.
