Frequently Asked Questions & Guide

How do I use IconCopy to copy emojis?

Using IconCopy is extremely simple. Just browse through our curated categories or use the instant live search bar to find the exact emoji or icon you need. Click on any emoji card, and it will instantly be saved to your system clipboard. You can then paste it anywhere using Ctrl+V or Cmd+V.

Are these emojis compatible with iOS, Android, and Windows?

Yes, absolutely! IconCopy utilizes standard Unicode characters. This ensures that any symbol, icon, or emoji you copy from our platform will render perfectly across all modern operating systems, web browsers, and social media platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

Does IconCopy track my clipboard data?

Your privacy is our utmost priority. IconCopy is a pure client-side utility tool. We only track global aggregate counts to display total platform interactions via safe serverless streams. Your personal selections, recently copied history, and clipboard contents are never sent to any server; they stay 100% sandboxed in your browser storage.
